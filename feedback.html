In my case, I am more productive when I am in the classroom. I am not saying that online class learning is not effective. It is just that I am more focused when I am in the classroom. I am more motivated to learn. I am more engaged in the discussion. I am more participative in the activities. I am more attentive to the teacher.
I am more comfortable when I am at home because I can focus without any distractions. I can manage my time well. I can do other things after my class. Online class learning is effective for me.
Hybrid learning is the best for me. I can learn from the classroom and at home. At school, I can clarify my questions with my teacher. At home, I can do my homework and study for my exams. Students should be given more freedom to choose how they want to learn after all every student are unique.
